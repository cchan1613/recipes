Ingredients:
 - 1 serving of italian spaghetti
 - 1 cup of organic kale 
 - 1/2 cup of organic walnut
 - 2 teaspoons of olive oil
 - salt and pepper

Directions:
 1. Bring three large cups of springwater to a boil.

 2. Add spaghetti in pot, boil for 5 minutes and stir slightly to avoid sticking together.

 3. Add kale and let the mixture cook for 5 minutes.

 4. Add walnut and cook for 30 seconds

 5. Drain water using colander.

 6. Place mixture in serving plate.

 7. Add olive oil, salt and pepper for taste.
